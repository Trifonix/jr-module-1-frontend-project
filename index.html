FAQs.

Alpine Bus Journeys?
Forget crowded cities! Alpine Bus Journeys are your comfy seat with panoramic windows to breathtaking mountain vistas, charming villages, and air so fresh it might make you yodel (please don't, unless you're outside).
What about seating options?
Comfy standard seats with great views! All offer a front-row seat to the greatest show on Earth – the Alps!
What is the Alpine Wallet?
Digital wallet for smooth ticket booking. Load it up and travel faster than downhill!
My Altitude Score?
Loyalty points for mountain road cruising! Priority boarding + bragging rights.
Why a Bus Station Fee?
Small fee for clean stations and mountain air. Sometimes covers rogue bicycles.
Why are buses late?
Weather, sheep, photo stops, or tractors. Welcome to Mountain Road Time!
Peak Season Surcharge?
Extra for sharing views with new friends. Guarantees vibrant journey!
